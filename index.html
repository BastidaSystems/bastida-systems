About

I am David Ramirez Bastida, founder of Bastida Systems LLC. My work combines multimedia design, UX/UI, software development, real-world operations, and AI-assisted tools to create practical systems that solve problems inside real businesses. My approach comes from direct experience in hospitality, casino, and kitchen environments, where manual processes, lack of traceability, and operational disorder can create losses, delays, and risks.

Through Bastida Systems, I develop products such as FiltraCore, BeoFlow, Shift Rotation, and La Apertura, combining design, technology, and business vision to build solutions with real value.
