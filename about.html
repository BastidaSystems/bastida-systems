About Bastida Systems

Building intelligent systems for real-world operations.

Bastida Systems develops intelligent software platforms focused on monitoring, automation, and operational reliability. Our systems transform complex infrastructure into clear, data-driven environments that improve visibility, reduce risk, and ensure compliance.

We specialize in building solutions for industries where failure is costly—such as casinos, hospitality, and industrial operations—where real-time insights and proactive maintenance are critical.

Our operations are supported by specialized areas led by experienced professionals. These include administration, software development, cybersecurity and safety compliance (OSHA), and marketing. Key leadership includes Rodrigo R. Bastida, Manager of Administration; D. Ramírez Bastida, overseeing Legal, Technology, Security, and Executive Operations; and Rafael Amezquita, Manager of Marketing and Advertising. This multidisciplinary structure ensures that every system we build is not only technically robust, but also aligned with operational, legal, and commercial standards.

Mission

To build intelligent systems that enhance operational visibility, reliability, and control through real-time data and automation.

Vision

To redefine how real-world operations are managed by transforming infrastructure into fully intelligent, data-driven ecosystems.
